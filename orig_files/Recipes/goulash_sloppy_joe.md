# Goulash / Sloppy Joe

## Ingredients
- Ground beef (1 lb for Sloppy Joe / ½ lb for Goulash)
- 1 lb elbow noodles
- 8 pack of hamburger buns
- 2-10 oz cans tomato soup (or 1-23 oz can)
- 1 teaspoon onion powder
- Salt & pepper (to taste)
- 2 Tablespoons sugar
- 2 Tablespoons white vinegar
- 3 squirts of ketchup

## Instructions

Prepare elbow noodles as directed. Brown ground beef. Add onion powder, salt & pepper. Drain. Add tomato soup plus 1 can of water per can of soup. Add sugar, vinegar, sugar, ketchup. Bring to a low boil. Cover and simmer for 20 minutes. Remove cover and simmer for a few minutes to thicken.

> **Note:** For Sloppy Joe, serve on hamburger buns. For Goulash, mix with elbow noodles.
