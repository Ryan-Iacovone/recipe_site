## Ingredients
- 1 lb chicken tenderloins (breast would work too)
- ½ Cup Panko breadcrumbs (add seasoning if desired)
- 2 tablespons butter
- 8 taco size tortillas
- 2 cups colby jack cheese
- Lettuce
- Ranch

## Instructions
1. Pound chicken. 
2. Cut chicken into strips or nuggets. 
3. Dip chicken in bowl of melted butter then dip in bowl of breadcrumbs. 
4. Place chicken on baking sheet and bake at 450° for 8 minutes, flip, then cook for 7 minutes 
5. Assemble wraps
