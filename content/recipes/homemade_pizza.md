## Ingredients
- Pillsbury thin pizza crust
- 1-14oz can pizza sauce
- Toppings (pepperoni, ham, mushrooms, pepper)
- 8 ozs (2 cups) shredded mozzarella cheese

## Instructions

Place pizza dough on cookie sheet, spread to edges. Cut off any excess dough. Pre-bake dough at 350° for 5 minutes. Add pizza sauce, any desired toppings, cheese. Bake at 350° until cheese is melted, approximately 15 minutes.
