## Ingredients
- Bread (plain or cinnamon)
- 4 eggs
- Cinnamon (if not using cinnamon bread)
- ½ cup milk
- Cooking spray
- Syrup

## Instructions

Cut bread slices in half. With fork stir eggs, milk and cinnamon. Whisk with fork to add air to egg mixture. Spray flat pan with cooking spray. Heat on medium-high heat. Dip bread slices in egg mixture. Place on flat pan. Cook until brown. Flip with spatula and brown other side.
