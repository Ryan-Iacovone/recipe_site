Lasagna

February 21, 2026

Ingredients#

  • 1 lb uncooked lasagna noodles (for 3 layers)
  • 1 lb ground beef (or less)
  • 2 teaspoons onion powder
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder
  • 2 Tablespoons minced parsley
  • 1½ Tablespoon oregano
  • 1-1/2 teaspoons salt
  • ½ teaspoon black pepper
  • (1) 12 oz can tomato paste (or (2) 6 oz cans)
  • 8 ozs (2 cups) grated mozzarella cheese
  • 8 ozs (2 cups) grated sharp cheddar cheese
  • ¾ cup parmesan cheese

Instructions#

Boil noodles as directed on box. Add oil so noodles don’t stick together. Drain. Cool.

Grate mozzarella cheese and sharp cheddar cheese and mix with parmesan cheese in a bowl.

Brown beef. Then add onion powder, garlic powder, parsley, oregano, salt & pepper to beef and stir until combined. Add tomato paste to beef mixture with 3 cans of water using tomato paste can. Bring to a low boil and simmer for 10 minutes.

In 13"x9" metal pan cover bottom of pan with meat sauce, then layer of noodles, layer of meat sauce, layer of cheese, layer of noodles, layer of meat sauce, layer of cheese, layer of noodles, layer of sauce, layer of cheese (3 total layers of noodles).

Cover pan with aluminum foil and bake at 350° for 20 minutes. Remove aluminum foil and bake until top is lightly brown and sauce bubbles up from the sides, around 10 minutes.
