## Ingredients
- 8 oz pork sausage
- dash onion powder
- 1 teaspoon minced garlic
- 1 red bell pepper, diced
- 1 green bell pepper, diced
- 12 eggs, beaten
- ½ teaspoon sea salt
- ¼ teaspoon pepper
- 1.5+ cup shredded cheddar cheese

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
2. Grease a 9×13" baking dish, set aside.
3. In a large skillet over medium heat, cook pork sausage.
4. Add garlic and onion powder along with bell peppers and cook until sausage begins to brown and veggies are soft.
5. Remove from heat and let cool.
6. In a medium bowl, whisk together eggs.
7. Add sea salt and pepper and whisk to combine.
8. Add cooled bacon and veggie mixture to the beaten eggs and stir to combine.
9. Add cheese and stir to combine.
10. Pour into prepared baking dish and spread the mixture evenly in the dish.
11. Bake in preheated oven for ~20 minutes, or until top is set and slightly browned.
12. Cool for 5 minutes and serve warm.
