## Ingredients
- 1 lb chicken (breast or tenderloins)
- 1-10 oz can Campbell's cream of chicken soup

## Instructions

Brown chicken. Add soup and 1 can of water using the soup can. Bring to a boil. Cover. Simmer for at least 30 minutes.
