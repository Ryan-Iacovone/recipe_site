## Small Red Potatoes

Wash and scrub potatoes thoroughly. Only cut super big potatoes so they are all about the same size.

Put potatoes into saucepan with enough water to cover all the potatoes. Heat potatoes until they are boiling. Then reduce heat to simmer and cover potatoes 15 to 20 minutes or until tender.

## Baked Potatoes — Russet Potatoes

### Oven Method
Preheat oven to 350°F. Thoroughly scrub and wash the potatoes. Poke potatoes with a fork. Coat the potatoes in vegetable oil and sprinkle a little salt over them. Wrap potatoes in aluminum foil. Put wrapped potatoes in oven for 1 hour and 15 minutes.

### Microwave Method
Thoroughly scrub and wash the potatoes. Poke potatoes with a fork. Put potato on microwave safe plate and cook for 5 minutes. Potentially have to cook longer; cut up potato into pieces for it to cook faster.
