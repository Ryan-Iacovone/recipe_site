## Sauteed Mushrooms

### Ingredients
- ½ lb (3 cups) mushrooms, sliced
- 1 Tablespoon butter
- ¼ Teaspoon salt
- ⅛ Teaspoon pepper
- 1 clove garlic, finely chopped (or ¼ Teaspoon garlic powder)
- Chopped parsley, if desired

### Instructions

In skillet over medium-high heat melt butter, add garlic, salt & pepper. Add mushrooms. Cook 4–6 minutes until mushrooms are light brown. Sprinkle with parsley.

---

## Caramelized Onions

### Ingredients
- Sweet onion, sliced
- 2 Tablespoons butter
- ¼ Teaspoon salt

### Instructions

In skillet melt butter over medium-high heat. Add onions and cook for 10 minutes. Reduce heat to medium-low. Add salt. Cook an additional 35–40 minutes until onions are deep golden brown.
