# Breakfast Quesadillas

## Ingredients
- 4 eggs, beaten
- Salt & pepper to taste
- ⅓ cup diced ham, crumbled bacon or sausage
- Mushrooms, chopped
- Onion, chopped
- 1 cup cheddar cheese or colby-jack cheese
- 4 flour tortillas (taco or fajita size)
- Salsa
- Sour cream
- Cooking spray

## Instructions

In large skillet scramble eggs, add salt & pepper to taste. Add meat, mushrooms, onion. Put ¼ of egg mixture and ¼ cup cheese on half of each tortilla and fold over. Spray skillet with cooking spray. Place quesadillas in skillet and cook over medium heat until golden brown. Flip and cook until other side is brown. Serve with salsa & sour cream.
