# Meatloaf

## Ingredients
- 1 lb ground beef
- ½ cup bread crumbs
- 1 egg
- 1 tsp onion powder
- Salt & Pepper (to taste)
- Splash of milk
- Squirt of ketchup
- Cooking spray

## Instructions

Mix all ingredients together in bowl with hands. Add more bread crumbs or milk for desired consistency. Spray loaf pan with cooking spray. Press meat mixture into loaf pan (or form into a rectangle on baking sheet). Cover with aluminum foil and bake at 350° for 30 minutes. Remove aluminum foil and cook for 30 minutes longer or until no longer pink in the middle and brown on top.
