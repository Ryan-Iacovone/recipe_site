## Ingredients
- 1 can (10½ ounces) Campbell's Condensed Cream of Mushroom Soup
- ½–¾ cup milk
- ⅛ teaspoon black pepper
- 2 cans (14½ ounces each) French-style Del Monte Green Beans, drained
- 1⅓ cups French's Original Crispy Fried Onions, divided
- ½ cup shredded cheddar cheese

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Mix cream of mushroom soup, milk, and pepper in a 1½-quart baking dish. Stir in green beans, ⅔ cup crispy fried onions, and cheese.
2. Bake 30 minutes or until hot. Stir.
3. Top with remaining ⅔ cup onions and cheese. Bake 5 minutes longer or until onions are golden brown.
