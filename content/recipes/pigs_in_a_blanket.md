## Ingredients
- 8 hot dogs
- 8 crescent rolls
- 4 cheese slices cut in half

## Instructions

With knife slice uncooked hot dogs down the middle to insert ½ slice of cheese. Roll crescent roll around each hot dog. Place on baking sheet. Bake at 350° until cheese is melted and rolls are slightly brown.
