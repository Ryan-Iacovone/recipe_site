# Loaded Mashed Potato Casserole (6+ servings)

## Ingredients
- 3 pounds russet potatoes, peeled and cut into 1-1/2" pieces
- ¾ cup milk
- 4 tablespoons butter, melted
- 1 cup sour cream
- Salt & pepper to taste
- 1-2/3 cups cheddar cheese, shredded
- ½ cup bacon (finely crumbled)
- Cooking spray
- Chopped chives, optional
- Chopped parsley, optional

## Instructions

Boil potatoes with 2 tablespoons salt for 15–20 minutes until tender. Drain potatoes. Mash potatoes with mixer. Stir in milk, butter, sour cream, salt & pepper. Stir in 1 cup cheddar cheese.

Spray 9" glass baking dish with cooking spray. Add potatoes. Top with remaining 2/3 cup cheddar cheese. Cover with aluminum foil.

Refrigerate for next day use or bake at 350° for 20 minutes. Remove foil. Add bacon. Bake, uncovered, for additional 20 minutes or until cheese is bubbly. Sprinkle with chives and parsley, if desired.
