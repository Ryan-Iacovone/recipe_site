## Ingredients
- 1 lb Ground beef
- Taco seasoning mix 25% less sodium (1 oz)
- 6 Flour tortillas (8-10 inches diameter)
- 1 Small tomato (roma or cherry) chopped (½ cup)
- 4 Medium green onions or 1 onion
- Or pico de gallo
- Black or pinto beans (16 oz)
- Colby jack or cheddar cheese (2 cups)
- Type of pepper
- Lettuce
- Sour cream

## Instructions

Cut/chop all vegetables appropriately. Brown ground beef with the possibility of putting vegetables in (drain). Add taco seasoning mix according to its directions. Heat up beans separately. Warm up taco shells in the microwave (20 seconds for 2 shells) by using slightly damp paper towels on both sides of taco shells. Ready for regular tacos so assemble meat, vegetables, cheese, lettuce, and beans.

**For quesadillas:** Set oven to 350°F. Set open faced tortillas on a cookie sheet and spread cheese over half of each tortilla. Top with remaining meat, vegetables, lettuce, and beans. Fold tortilla over the filling. Bake for about 5 minutes in the oven or until the quesadilla is hot and cheese is melted.

**For leftovers:** Combine meat, vegetables, and beans into a singular bowl to put in the refrigerator. Reheat in the microwave prior to using this mixture for quesadillas.
