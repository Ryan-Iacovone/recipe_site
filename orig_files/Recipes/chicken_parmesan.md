# Chicken Parmesan

## Ingredients
- 1 lb chicken breast or tenderloin
- ¾ cup seasoned bread crumbs
- ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese
- 2 T butter, melted (or olive oil)
- ¾ cup mozzarella cheese
- 1 cup spaghetti sauce
- Cooking spray
- Spaghetti noodles

## Instructions

Pound chicken. Melt butter in bowl. Combine breadcrumbs and parmesan cheese in another bowl. Dip chicken in butter then heavily dip in breadcrumbs. Place chicken on baking sheet. Spray top of chicken with cooking spray. Bake in 450° oven for 25 minutes, flipping chicken halfway. Spoon spaghetti sauce over chicken. Top with mozzarella cheese. Bake 5 more minutes until cheese is melted.

> **Note:** Watch time to cook chicken.
