## Ingredients
- 1 lb chicken (breast or tenderloins)
- ½ Cup Panko breadcrumbs (add seasoning if desired)
- 2 T butter, melted

## Instructions

Pound chicken. Cut chicken into strips or nuggets. Melt butter. Dip chicken in melted butter then dip in breadcrumbs. Place chicken on baking sheet and bake at 450° for 5 minutes (5 minutes for nuggets, longer for strips). Flip chicken. Bake an additional 5 minutes.
