## Ingredients
- 1 pound lean ground turkey
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon pepper
- ¼ teaspoon garlic powder
- 1 tablespoon olive oil

## Instructions

1. Preheat the air fryer to 350°F (180°C).
2. In a large bowl, add the ground turkey, salt, pepper, garlic powder, and olive oil. Mix everything together until just combined.
3. Lightly wet your hands and shape the mixture into four turkey burger patties. Press down in the center of each patty.
4. Add the turkey burgers to a greased air fryer basket and air fry for 12 minutes, flipping halfway through. Once the burgers reach an internal temperature of 165°F, remove them from the air fryer.
5. Let the burgers rest for a minute or two before assembling.

## Notes

- **To store**: Keep leftovers in the refrigerator, covered, for up to five days.
- **To freeze**: Place the cooked and cooled burgers in a shallow container and store in the freezer for up to 6 months.
- **Other ground meat**: Ground chicken and ground beef will work the same.
