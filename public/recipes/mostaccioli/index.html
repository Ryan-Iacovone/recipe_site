Mostaccioli

February 21, 2026

Ingredients#

  • 1 lb penne noodles
  • ½ lb ground beef
  • 1-24 oz Prego sauce
  • 8 ozs (2 cups) shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Red and/or green peppers, chopped (optional)
  • Pepper, onion (optional)

Instructions#

Cook noodles as directed. Drain. Brown ground beef. Drain beef, if necessary. Add peppers, if desired, to beef mixture and cook for several minutes. Place ground beef mixture in large bowl. Add cheese to ground beef mixture and mix together. Add sauce to beef mixture and mix together. Add noodles to desired consistency. Put in 8"x8" baking pan (or 9x13 pan if using all noodles). Cover baking pan with aluminum foil. Bake at 350° for 30 minutes. Remove aluminum foil from top of baking pan. Stir and bake until bubbly around 10 minutes.
