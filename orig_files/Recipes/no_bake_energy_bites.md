## Ingredients
- 1 cup old-fashioned oats
- ⅔ cup toasted (350° for 5 minutes) shredded coconut (sweetened or unsweetened)
- ½ cup creamy peanut butter
- ½ cup ground flaxseed
- ½ cup semisweet mini chocolate chips (or vegan chocolate chips)
- ⅓ cup honey
- 1 T chia seeds (optional)
- 1 t vanilla extract

## Instructions

Stir all ingredients together in large mixing bowl until thoroughly combined. Cover and chill in refrigerator for 1–2 hours. Make mixture into balls (or press mixture into square baking dish). Refrigerate in sealed container for up to 1 week.
