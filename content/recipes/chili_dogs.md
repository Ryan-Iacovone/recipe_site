## Ingredients
- Hot dogs
- Hot dog buns
- 10 oz Hormel No Beans Chili
- Shredded cheddar cheese

## Instructions

Boil hot dogs as directed. Heat no beans chili until heated through. Place hot dog on bun, add chili, cheese. Cook at 425° until cheese is melted.
