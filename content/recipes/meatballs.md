## Ingredients
- 1 lb ground beef
- ⅓ cup Progresso Italian style bread crumbs
- 1 egg
- ½ tsp salt (or ½ tsp garlic)
- ½ tsp pepper
- 1 tsp Mrs. Dash seasoning (Italian or Garlic & Herbs)
- ⅓ cup parmesan cheese
- 2-24 oz Prego spaghetti sauce
- Cooking spray
- 16oz thin spaghetti 

> **Note:** If using green pepper, put it in sauce to cook instead of sauté.

## Instructions

Add bread crumbs to ground beef mix with a few sprinkles of water to help hold together and roll into meatballs.

Coat 9"x9" baking pan with cooking spray, add meatballs. Cook at 325° for 15 minutes. Flip meatballs over and cook for an additional 15 minutes. Remove meatballs from pan and add to spaghetti sauce in saucepan. Bring to a boil. Cover and simmer for 2–3 hours.

Boil spaghetti as box directs
