## Ingredients
- ¼ cup butter, cubed
- ¼ cup flour
- 2 tsp salt
- ½ tsp pepper
- 2½ cup milk
- 1½ cup shredded Velveeta
- 6 medium potatoes, thinly sliced

## Instructions

In a large saucepan, melt butter. Whisk the flour, salt, and pepper until smooth.

Gradually add milk bringing it to a boil. Cook and stir for 2 minutes or until thickened.

Reduce heat, add cheese and cook and stir until all is melted.

Place potatoes in a greased 13x9 baking dish. Pour sauce over potatoes.

Bake uncovered at 350°F for 1 hour or until potatoes are tender.
