## Ingredients
- 1 Cup white sugar
- 1 Cup brown sugar
- 1 Cup unsalted butter (2 sticks of butter - softened)
- 2 large eggs
- 1 tsp Baking soda
- 2 tsp Vanilla extract
- 2 tsp Hot water
- ½ tsp Salt
- 3 cups all-purpose flour
- 2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips
- 2 cups peanut butter chips
- 1 cup chopped walnuts

## Instructions

Add both the white sugar and brown sugar to your mixer bowl. Make sure you use a large enough bowl.

Cream together the butter and sugar mixture with your mixer at low speed until fairly smooth. Add eggs into mixture 1 at a time, mixing for 10-15 seconds each. Stir in Vanilla while mixing. Dissolve baking soda with hot water. Stir in baking soda mixture with a spoon. While mixing, sprinkle in salt.

Stir in flour, half a cup at a time with mixer on medium speed. Stir in chocolate chips, walnuts, and peanut butter chips half a cup at a time.

**(Optional)** If you like rounder cookies, wait to start preheating oven until you've chilled the dough for half an hour, cover the bowl with plastic wrap and chill in the fridge for 30 minutes.

Use a tablespoon to scoop rounded dough balls onto ungreased pan or a parchment lined pan. Bake cookies for 11 minutes at 350°.
