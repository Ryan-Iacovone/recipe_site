## Ingredients
- 1 lb ground beef
- Two 10 oz cans red enchilada sauce (2 hot or 1 hot, 1 mild)
- One 4 oz can chopped green chiles
- Refried beans
- 8 flour tortillas (fajita or taco size, fajita are smaller)
- 2+ cups cheddar cheese
- Sour cream (for serving)
- Tortilla chips as side

## Instructions

Brown ground beef. Drain. Stir in ½ cup of enchilada sauce and green chiles. Simmer for 2–3 minutes. Spread ½ cup enchilada sauce in bottom of 13x9" pan. Spread ¼ cup beef mixture down center of each tortilla, spread beans, sprinkle with cheese. Wrap tortilla tightly around filling, placing seam down in baking pan. Top with remaining enchilada sauce. Sprinkle with remaining cheese.

Bake 10–15 minutes at 375° until bubbly.
