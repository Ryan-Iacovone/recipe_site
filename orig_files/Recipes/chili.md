# Chili

## Ingredients
- 1 lb ground beef
- 2 tsp onion powder
- ¼ tsp garlic powder
- Salt & pepper to taste
- Tomato sauce (29 ounces)
- Chili beans (40 ounces) (Brooks hot chili beans)
- Cheddar cheese (for serving)
- Elbow noodles (optional)
- Pepper (optional)

## Instructions

Brown ground beef. Add onion powder, garlic powder, salt & pepper, tomato sauce, beans. Bring to a boil. Cover. Simmer for 1 hour. Serve with cheddar cheese.
