## Ingredients
- Wide egg noodles
- 1-10 oz cream of mushroom soup
- 1-5 oz can tuna in water, drained
- Splash of milk
- Salt & Pepper to taste
- Bread crumbs

## Instructions

Cook egg noodles as directed. Drain. Add soup, tuna, milk, salt & pepper. Bring to a boil then simmer for 5 minutes. Sprinkle with bread crumbs.
