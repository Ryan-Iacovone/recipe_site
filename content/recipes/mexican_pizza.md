## Ingredients
- 1-2 burrito size tortilla shells
- 1 lb ground beef
- 1-10 oz can hot enchilada sauce
- 1-16 oz can refried beans
- 1 medium onion, chopped (or green onion)
- 2 cups (8 oz) shredded colby-jack cheese

## Instructions

Brown ground beef. Add enchilada sauce to ground beef and heat until boiling. Heat refried beans in microwave or on stovetop. On baking sheet place tortilla shells. Spread refried beans on tortilla shells. Layer on ground beef mixture. Layer onions, layer cheese. Bake at 425° for 5–7 minutes or until cheese is melted.
