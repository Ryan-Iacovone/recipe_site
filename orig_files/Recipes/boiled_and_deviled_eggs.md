# Boiled and Deviled Eggs

## Ingredients (for 6 eggs)
- 6 eggs
- 3 tablespoons of mayonnaise
- ½ teaspoon ground mustard or small regular mustard
- ⅛ teaspoon salt
- ⅛ teaspoon pepper

## Instructions

### Boiled Eggs
Put eggs in a saucepan. Completely cover eggs with cold water. Heat on high until boiling. Once rolling boil, remove pan from heat and cover. Let stand for 15 minutes. After, add cold water to cool eggs off. Once eggs are cool, peel.

### Deviled Eggs
Once eggs are peeled, cut lengthwise in half. Take out yolks and put them into a small bowl. Mash yolk with a fork. Stir mayonnaise, mustard, salt, and pepper into yolks. Fill whites with the egg yolk mixture.

---

### Air Fryer Boiled Eggs
Cook at 270° for 15 minutes. Cool immediately after cooking. Keep in fridge up to 7 days.
